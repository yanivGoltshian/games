# Visual asset provenance

The app never hotlinks visual assets. The four photographs below are cropped,
resized, and encoded locally as WebP files for offline use. All remaining
vocabulary, portals, environments, controls, mascot, and reward artwork is
original project artwork.

| Local file | Source | Creator / credit | License | Changes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `public/assets/vocabulary/apple.webp` | [Fuji apple](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Fuji_apple.jpg) | Scott Bauer, USDA Agricultural Research Service | Public domain, U.S. federal government work | Cropped to one foreground apple, resized to 640×640, encoded as WebP |
| `public/assets/vocabulary/duck.webp` | [Mallard at Seedskadee National Wildlife Refuge](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Mallard_at_Seedskadee_National_Wildlife_Refuge_-_Flickr_-_USFWS_Mountain_Prairie.jpg) | USFWS Mountain-Prairie | Public domain, U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service work | Resized to 768 px wide, encoded as WebP |
| `public/assets/vocabulary/bird.webp` | [American robin](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:American_robin_(46946243164).jpg) | Courtney Celley / USFWS Midwest Region | Public domain, U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service work | Cropped around the single bird, resized to 640×640, encoded as WebP |
| `public/assets/vocabulary/sun.webp` | [Full disk of the Sun](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Full_disk_of_the_Sun_(eso1703e-comparisona).jpg) | ESO using NASA/SDO Atmospheric Imaging Assembly data | [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/) | Resized to 768×768, encoded as WebP |

not legally required. No photograph is used to imply endorsement by its source.
